4. Yayasan LIA

Yayasan LIA is a learning institute that provides educational services with language as its core business. Yayasan LIA upholds its motto "A Small Step Toward the Nation's Intellectual Enhancement". Established on September 7, 1959, Yayasan LIA started out with the name Lembaga Indonesia Amerika, to be changed later to Perhimpunan Persahabatan Indonesia Amerika, shortened to PPIA. LIA started its first activity by opening English classes for around 40 college students, a number which at present stands at approximately 60.000 per team spread in 70 outlets in 16 Provinces in Indonesia.
